Controls as of Sept 9 2014
-move the camera with WASD
-zoom with the mousewheel
-left click to select a unit (Currently no units on the map due to testing map generation)

Debug keys:
-"r" to reset the map; it will generate a new map (WILL USE UP ALOT OF RAM)
-"k" to zoom out to the max, showing the entire map
